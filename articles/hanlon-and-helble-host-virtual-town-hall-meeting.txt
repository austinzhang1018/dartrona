President Philip J. Hanlon '77 and Provost Joseph Helble will host a virtual town hall meeting on Wednesday, March 18, at 3 p.m., to update the community on the latest information regarding Dartmouth's response to the COVID-19 pandemic and answer questions.
"In this time of great uncertainty for our country and the world, I am gratified by how quickly the Dartmouth community has acted to protect one another's health and well-being and to adapt our teaching and research mission and supporting work to the reality of this global pandemic," says President Hanlon.
